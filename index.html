The 20 Richest Technology Billionaires in 2021

According to Forbes, a leading business magazine, stock markets have rallied since the Covid-19 boom, helping to push the fortunes of the world's billionaires to record highs. But no ultra-rich group has benefited as much as the tech billionaires - who add $2.5 trillion to Forbes' 2021 World Billionaires list, an increase of about 80% from the $1.4 trillion in the last year.
